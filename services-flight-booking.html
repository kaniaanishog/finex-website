Flight Booking & Travel Support

Seamless ticketing and travel planning for students, families and business travellers, coordinated with your forex, insurance and loan timelines.

← Back to all services

What we help you with

  • One-way and return flight bookings for domestic and international travel
  • Student and family travel planning aligned with university reporting dates
  • Business trip itineraries with flexible rescheduling options
  • Coordination with forex issuance and travel insurance start dates

How our travel support works

  1. Travel requirement discussion: Destination, dates, baggage and special requirements.
  2. Fare and route options: We share multiple options balancing cost, time and convenience.
  3. Booking & confirmation: Tickets are booked and shared along with basic visa/immigration checks where relevant.
  4. Last-mile support: Changes, rescheduling and coordination if linked with loans, forex or insurance.

Who is this for?

Students flying out for studies, families visiting abroad, business owners and professionals travelling for work.

Details we’ll need

  • Full names as per passport
  • Travel dates and preferred time windows
  • Departure and arrival cities
  • Any airline preferences or baggage needs
