Student & SOP Assistance

End-to-end support for students and parents – from SOP drafting and budgeting to education loans, forex and insurance for studies abroad.

← Back to all services

What we help you with

  • Brainstorming, drafting and polishing Statements of Purpose (SOPs)
  • Basic financial planning for studies – tuition, stay, insurance and buffers
  • Education loan planning and lender comparison
  • Forex cards and currency for students
  • Health and travel insurance for overseas study

Our student support flow

  1. Profile & goal discussion: Course, country and long-term plans.
  2. SOP structure & drafting: We help you bring out your story while keeping it genuine and clear.
  3. Finance & loan planning: Estimate total cost and identify suitable loan and forex options.
  4. Pre-departure checklist: Insurance, forex and basic money management tips before you leave.

Who is this for?

Students planning to study abroad and parents who want a single point of contact for money-related decisions around education.

Helpful documents & details

  • Academic transcripts and test scores
  • Offer letters / admission confirmations
  • Rough budget or fee structure
  • Passport and basic KYC for loans/forex
