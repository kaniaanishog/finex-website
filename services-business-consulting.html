Business & Startup Consulting

From idea to execution, we help you structure your business, get your finances in order and make clear, numbers-based decisions.

← Back to all services

What we help you with

  • Choosing the right business structure (proprietorship, partnership, LLP, company)
  • Registrations and basic compliance roadmap
  • Business plans and financial projections
  • Project reports and loan proposal preparation
  • Cash flow and working capital planning
  • Designing simple SOPs for finance-related processes

How our consulting works

  1. Discovery meeting: Understand your idea, current stage and challenges.
  2. Diagnostic & roadmap: Identify key focus areas and create a practical action plan.
  3. Implementation support: Assist with registrations, documentation and coordination with other professionals.
  4. Ongoing review: Periodic check-ins to refine plans as your business grows.

Who is this for?

New entrepreneurs, small business owners and startups who want clarity on how to structure their business and finances from day one.

Good to have ready

  • Brief note on your business idea and target customers
  • Current revenue/cost details (if already running)
  • Any existing registrations or licenses
